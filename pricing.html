ACCESS & PRICING

Hardware plus platform, scoped to your site.

Every deployment is a Mk.II Node (or several) plus access to the monitoring platform and compliance portal. We scope to the number of nodes, the monitoring period and the limits that apply — then quote it plainly.

HOW ACCESS WORKS

Three ways to run it

Single node

One node on one location. Noise, vibration or seismic with portal access and signed reports. Ideal for a focused monitoring requirement or a short programme.

Site

Multiple nodes across one project, on a shared fleet map with per-site limits, role-based access for your team and the client, and scheduled reporting.

Managed fleet

Nodes across many projects with multi-tenant isolation, OTA firmware management, retention controls and Prometheus metrics into your own stack.

HOW WE QUOTE

Three rules

You pay for nodes and the monitoring period.

Pricing follows the number of nodes on site and how long they run, plus platform access. No per-report fees, no surprises when an auditor asks for an export.

Pilots are short, scoped, and measurable.

Put a node on a real location for a defined period against a written success measure. If it does not give you the record you need, you do not continue.

The evidence is yours.

Your readings and reports belong to you. Exports are available throughout, and data retention is configurable to your policy.
