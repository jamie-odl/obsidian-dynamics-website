EARLY-WARNING APP

Vela

Early disruption awareness for flights and airports. Vela combines official operational signals with structured first-hand reports so travelers can act sooner.

Primary Job

Help users see disruption building early, before delays become obvious in standard travel apps.

Signal Model

Blends weather, airport operations, traffic, and structured on-site reports into practical risk guidance.

User Outcome

Faster, calmer decisions on when to leave, what to monitor, and how to share status with others.
