FAQ

SkyGrid vs Blackglass

Both are Obsidian surfaces; this site explains how they divide ownership without inventing extra product lines.

When is Project SkyGrid the right entry point?

When your primary gap is air-domain anomaly intelligence in the browser — watch areas, detection classes, and workflow-ready signals before disruption compounds.

When does Blackglass make sense?

When you need a fleet-grade console for baselines, drift, evidence exports, and subscription-scoped access alongside governed SaaS controls.

Do I buy both?

Not always. Many teams start with one surface, prove value, then bundle when both air-domain signals and fleet governance are required. Platform & access outlines tiers.

Where do I use the live dashboard?

Project SkyGrid operates at projectskygrid.com. Blackglass is provisioned per customer — use Contact for access discussions.
