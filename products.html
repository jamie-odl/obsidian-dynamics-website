QUESTIONS

How it works

Where is data evaluated?

Each node evaluates thresholds on-site, so a breach is detected even if the network drops. Readings then stream to the cloud platform for storage, dashboards and reporting, with any buffered data backfilled on reconnection.

Which standards are supported?

The platform ships limit profiles aligned to BS 5228 for construction noise and BS 7385 for building vibration, with separate day, evening and night thresholds and working-hours filtering. Limits are configurable per site to match your consent.

What evidence can I export?

The compliance portal produces signed PDF reports and CSV exports with hash-stamped integrity — suitable for consultants, clients and regulators. Reports can be scheduled or generated on demand.

How are firmware updates handled?

Firmware is delivered over the air with staged rollout and rollback. A whole fleet can be updated without a single site visit, and node health is visible throughout.

Is it multi-tenant?

Yes. Every query, export and dashboard is scoped to its organisation. You can isolate clients, projects and sites while operating a single fleet.
