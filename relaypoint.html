METHODOLOGY

How propagation risk is scored

Dependency Graph

Nodes and corridors are mapped into a dependency graph used to estimate spillover pathways.

Confidence Model

Scores combine baseline deviation, source signal confidence, and handoff criticality weighting.

False Positive Guardrails

Time-window checks and corroboration rules suppress low-signal chain reactions.
