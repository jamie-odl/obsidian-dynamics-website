About

We organise developer contribution records from published UK local authority documents — so researchers, journalists, and operators can follow every figure back to source.

What we do

Obsidian Dynamics Limited builds intelligence on Section 106 planning obligations, Community Infrastructure Levy (CIL), and Infrastructure Funding Statements (IFS). We extract, structure, and provenance-link figures that are already in the public domain.

Outputs are for research and operational review. They are not legal, planning, or financial advice — always verify against the original authority documents.
