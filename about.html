ABOUT

Obsidian Dynamics Limited

A UK software company building developer contributions intelligence from published Infrastructure Funding Statements and related public records.

What we do

We extract, structure, and link developer contribution data — Section 106 planning obligations, Community Infrastructure Levy receipts, and Infrastructure Funding Statement tables — back to the source documents authorities publish. Where extraction is uncertain, we flag it rather than guess.

The intelligence workspace covers authority search, delivery pressure analysis, FOI tracking, and evidence exports. Access is by invitation for researchers and operational users. Journalists can use our Research & data page and public API at /api/public/coverage without an account.

Outputs are for review and research. They are not legal, financial, or planning advice. Always verify figures against the original published documents.
