ABOUT US

Obsidian Dynamics

We build operational risk intelligence systems for the physical world, helping teams detect disruption early, assess impact clearly, and act before delays cascade.

OUR MISSION

One Thesis. Built End-to-End.

Obsidian Dynamics is the parent company behind SkyGrid and Strait-Signal, with Atlas as the enterprise overlay layer across platform signals — one operating model from telemetry to decisions.

We focus on practical outcomes for operators, analysts, and insurers: earlier warning, clearer triage, and faster decisions under uncertainty. The goal is not more data; it is better operational judgement at the moment it matters.

Our systems are built for cross-domain reality: what starts as an air anomaly or maritime chokepoint event often propagates through transfer networks. That is why we treat detection, scoring, and propagation as one coherent intelligence problem.

FOUNDER-LED

Technical Leadership, Operational Discipline

Obsidian Dynamics is founder-led and engineering-heavy by design. Core systems, data pipelines, scoring logic, and delivery workflows are built in-house for reliability and auditability.

The company was incorporated in August 2025 to formalise a single strategic direction: detect disruption in air and maritime networks, then model where it propagates next with a shared risk vocabulary.

Every product decision is held to the same standard: does it improve real-world operational decisions? If not, we do not ship it.

PRINCIPLES

How We Build

Data Over Opinion

Every detection, alert, and risk score is derived from observable sensor data. No speculation, no editorial. Analysts make the final call — our platforms provide the evidence.

Advisory, Not Deterministic

Our detections are advisory signals, not final verdicts. We respect the complexity of real-world operations by flagging anomalies for human review, not making automated judgements.

API-First Architecture

Everything we build is API-first. Dashboards are important, but the real value is in structured data delivery — webhooks, REST endpoints, and machine-readable payloads that plug into existing workflows.

Operational Tempo

Intelligence that arrives late is noise. Our systems are designed for 15-minute data cycles and sub-second event routing. Speed is a feature, not a bonus.

Baseline-Driven

Static thresholds break. Our platforms compute rolling baselines from historical data and flag deviations, adapting to seasonal patterns, long-term trends, and evolving operational norms.

Transparency

Every alert includes context: what triggered it, what the baseline was, and what data sources contributed. Black-box intelligence has no place in critical operations.
