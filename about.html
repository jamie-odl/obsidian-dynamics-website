ABOUT US

Obsidian Dynamics

We build data platforms that give operations teams structured intelligence on aircraft anomalies and maritime chokepoint disruptions — before they cascade into costly delays.

OUR MISSION

Intelligence That Arrives First

Obsidian Dynamics is the parent company behind Project SkyGrid and Strait Signal — two mission-critical data platforms that close the gap between when a disruption forms and when the people who need to act on it find out.

Whether it's an aircraft holding a suspicious loiter pattern over critical infrastructure or a queue of 400 vessels building at a canal approach 8,000 miles away, our platforms deliver structured, validated intelligence before it becomes breaking news. At the parent level, we unify both data streams into a single enterprise intelligence layer.

We don't sell dashboards. We sell the hours of lead time that let operations teams make decisions instead of react to them.

FOUNDER-LED

Technical From the Top

Obsidian Dynamics is a technical founder-led company. Both platforms — from event-driven data pipeline architecture to the anomaly detection algorithms — are designed and built in-house. No outsourced development teams. No off-the-shelf dashboards rebranded as "intelligence."

The company was incorporated in August 2025 to formalise what began as two independent data engineering projects: one tracking aircraft anomalies using global ADS-B data, the other computing disruption baselines across seven maritime chokepoints from raw AIS position reports.

Both platforms share a common philosophy: ingest live sensor data, compute baselines, detect deviations, and deliver structured intelligence fast enough for operational use. Every architectural decision prioritises speed, auditability, and analyst trust.

PRINCIPLES

How We Build

Data Over Opinion

Every detection, alert, and risk score is derived from observable sensor data. No speculation, no editorial. Analysts make the final call — our platforms provide the evidence.

Advisory, Not Deterministic

Our detections are advisory signals, not final verdicts. We respect the complexity of real-world operations by flagging anomalies for human review, not making automated judgements.

API-First Architecture

Everything we build is API-first. Dashboards are important, but the real value is in structured data delivery — webhooks, REST endpoints, and machine-readable payloads that plug into existing workflows.

Operational Tempo

Intelligence that arrives late is noise. Our systems are designed for 15-minute data cycles and sub-second event routing. Speed is a feature, not a bonus.

Baseline-Driven

Static thresholds break. Our platforms compute rolling baselines from historical data and flag deviations, adapting to seasonal patterns, long-term trends, and evolving operational norms.

Transparency

Every alert includes context: what triggered it, what the baseline was, and what data sources contributed. Black-box intelligence has no place in critical operations.
