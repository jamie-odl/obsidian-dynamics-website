Obsidian Dynamics Limited builds tools for demanding environments. Obsidian Node helps teams track tagged assets on site — with a phone portal and industrial edge gateways that keep scanning in the background.

Our story

We started with the same obsession that shaped the Obsidian brand — gear and systems that survive real conditions. Lost or misplaced assets cost money and time whether you run a yard, a campus, a temp event, or a remote site.

Obsidian Node combines passive tags, industrial edge gateways, and a mobile portal so field teams can peel, stick, scan, and locate — without becoming IT experts.

The public website, customer portal, and operations console live on one platform at weareobsidian.co.uk — one company, one field-ready stack.
