Getting your access code

Each pilot customer receives an individual access code from Obsidian. After you create a portal account, enter your code on the activation screen to open your site workspace.

  1. Create your portal account with your work email.
  2. Enter the access code from your welcome email (for example OBS-PILOT-ACME).
  3. Open the asset portal and start scanning tags.
