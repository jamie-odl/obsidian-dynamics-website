WHY DRIFT MATTERS

Why drift matters

Firewall rules change. Packages update. Someone toggles PermitRootLogin. Manual spreadsheets and quarterly scans miss the window where risk actually changes. Small teams need the same signal clarity as multi-region fleets — without another heavyweight CMDB project.

Config drift you only notice when something breaks — or when an auditor asks.

Scattered SSH keys and inconsistent hardening across hosts make blast radius hard to scope.

Ad-hoc emergency fixes leave snowflake hosts nobody can explain or reproduce.

No clear evidence trail for security reviews — screenshots in Slack threads do not scale.

HOW IT WORKS

How it works

01

Connect hosts

Agentless SSH collection scoped to metadata you approve — no secret exfiltration. Push-agent installer script available for restricted environments.

02

Freeze baselines

Pin known-good snapshots after change freezes or releases. Run scans on demand or on schedule.

03

Detect drift

Surface sshd, sysctl, user/listener deltas with severity — not noisy noise. OCSF-formatted events for downstream tooling.

04

Remediate & prove

Track acknowledgement, ownership, and export audit-ready bundles. The remediator workflow drives resolution from detection to closed evidence.
