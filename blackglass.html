What Blackglass does on the Linux fleet

Your deployment, agents, SSH policies, and integrations stay private. This page states outcomes — specifics sit under NDA or contract.

SSH posture

Audit and track SSH posture across servers so weak or drifting access settings are visible before they become an incident or audit finding.

Linux baseline drift

Know when configuration moves away from the baseline you expect on your Linux hosts — with enough context for ops and security to agree on what changed.

Hardening workflow

One clear path for ops and security to prioritise fixes, prove hardening progress, and export evidence for reviews — fewer ad-hoc ticket threads and screenshots.

Tenant isolation

Boundaries and access rules match how your organisation segregates environments, regions, and duties.

Seats and entitlements

Who can remediate versus review is enforced in-product; packaging follows your commercial agreement.
