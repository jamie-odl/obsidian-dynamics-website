SECURITY & GOVERNANCE

Blackglass

A multi-tenant console for organisational security posture: agree what “good” looks like across assets and controls, catch meaningful drift, export review-ready evidence for audits and security reviews, and enforce subscription-scoped access — without treating the product like a flight-operations or air-traffic screen. The live surface is blackglasssec.com. Air-domain intelligence is a different product: Project SkyGrid at projectskygrid.com.

CAPABILITIES

What Blackglass is for

Your deployment, credentials, and integrations stay private. This page states scope and outcomes — implementation detail sits under NDA or contract.

Security baselines

Anchor reviews on a shared definition of expected configuration and ownership across systems, instead of reconciling tickets and spreadsheets after the fact.

Posture drift

Detect meaningful change with enough context for security and IT owners to agree what broke, what changed, and what to do next.

Evidence exports

Produce defensible packs for security reviews, audits, and internal governance — fewer one-off screenshots that do not hold up under scrutiny.

Tenant isolation

Multi-tenant boundaries and access rules match how your organisation actually segregates environments and duties.

Seats and entitlements

Who can change posture versus read-only review is reflected in-product; commercial packaging follows your agreement.

Optional SkyGrid context

Where you also run Project SkyGrid, air-domain signals can inform operational risk — Blackglass does not replace airspace monitoring and is not framed around flight displays.
