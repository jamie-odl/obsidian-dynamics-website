# Obsidian Dynamics Email Template Library

## Purpose

This library defines parent-brand outbound email standards for Obsidian Dynamics portfolio outreach.
It aligns parent positioning with product-specific pathways for Project SkyGrid and Strait-Signal.

## Positioning Rules

- Lead with cross-domain operational risk and executive decision velocity.
- Keep Obsidian Dynamics as the HERO brand and orchestration layer.
- Describe SkyGrid and Strait-Signal as standalone sister products under shared governance.
- Distinguish sample API surfaces from live enterprise firehose access.
- Use concise executive language and one primary CTA.

## Approved Templates

- `email-templates/01-core-enterprise-outreach.html`
  - Audience: Enterprise buyers with cross-domain operations
  - Purpose: Core platform outreach and technical discovery
- `email-templates/02-executive-brief-invite.html`
  - Audience: COO, Risk, and Operations leadership
  - Purpose: Book executive architecture and value briefing
- `email-templates/03-monthly-portfolio-digest.html`
  - Audience: Active opportunities and current customers
  - Purpose: Portfolio update tied to modules, reports, and dashboards

## Suggested Subject Lines

- Unified operational risk intelligence for air and maritime exposure
- Executive briefing invite: standalone modules, unified control plane
- Monthly portfolio digest: cross-domain signal and decision readiness

## Outreach Segments

- Enterprise operations and control tower programs
- Insurance and risk analytics leadership
- Infrastructure and logistics command teams
- Strategic platform and OEM partnership targets

## CTA Defaults

- Parent outreach: `Book Core discovery call`
- Executive motion: `Schedule executive briefing`
- Ongoing motion: `Review portfolio update`
