SANDBOX + REPLAY

Credentials workflow and webhook replay guidance

Sandbox Credentials

Open sandbox API scope via account operations. Sandbox keys remain isolated from operational routing and billing impact.

Operational Activation

Production keys are issued after endpoint validation, webhook verification, and replay sign-off.

Webhook Replay Pattern

Use GET /v1/history/replay with your source event window, then replay delivery to staging endpoints before go-live.

Idempotency Rule

Replay handlers must key by event_id and ignore duplicates to support retry/replay safely.
