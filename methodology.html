METHODOLOGY WHITEPAPER

How we turn telemetry into operational risk signals

A plain-English methodology for data provenance, confidence scoring, false-positive handling, and known system limitations.

Data Provenance

Inputs are ingested from authenticated sources, normalized into one schema, and timestamped for auditable processing lineage.

Confidence Scoring

Each event includes confidence derived from signal completeness, baseline deviation, corroborating context, and source quality.

False Positive Handling

Known benign patterns are documented; low-confidence events are surfaced with flags; operator feedback feeds threshold tuning.

Known Limitations

Coverage gaps, source latency constraints, and non-observable conditions are explicitly documented in every module workflow.

Governance Posture

Signals are advisory decision support outputs, not deterministic truth claims, and are intended to be interpreted by trained operators.

Update Cadence

Rule and model updates are versioned; significant changes are communicated with impact notes and rollout windows.
