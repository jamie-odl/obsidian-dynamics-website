HOW IT WORKS

From Raw Signal to Actionable Intelligence

Three stages. Fully automated. Intelligence delivered in minutes, not days.

01

Ingest

We ingest live ADS-B transponder feeds and AIS vessel position data globally — millions of position reports per hour across aviation and maritime domains.

02

Detect

Pattern-based anomaly detection compares live behaviour against rolling 7/30/90/365-day baselines. Loiter patterns, ghost tracks, queue buildups, rerouting surges — flagged automatically.

03

Deliver

Structured, validated intelligence delivered via interactive dashboards, REST APIs, or webhooks directly into Slack, Teams, or your existing operations toolchain.

Ever Given — Suez Canal Blockage

When the Ever Given grounded in March 2021, anchorage queues at both canal approaches began building hours before major outlets reported the blockage. Strait Signal's queue-tracking and delay-delta baselines would have flagged the anomaly within the first data cycle — giving logistics teams a critical head start to reroute or hold cargo.
