What we cover

Developer contributions from published records

Material already in the public domain — Infrastructure Funding Statements, Section 106 agreements, and Community Infrastructure Levy schedules. For research and review, not legal or planning advice.

Section 106

Planning obligations from published IFS tables — contributions received, spent, and outstanding, with category breakdowns where authorities report them.

CIL

Community Infrastructure Levy receipts and allocations as reported in Infrastructure Funding Statements, normalised across different reporting formats.

IFS

Statutory annual reports on developer contributions — extracted, linked to source pages, and flagged where figures need review.
