WHAT IS OBSIDIAN

One company, one platform thesis, clear product scope.

SkyGrid and Strait-Signal solve domain problems. Atlas unifies those signals into enterprise dependency, cascade-risk, and action workflows.

Who

Operations teams, risk and insurance leaders, and integration teams that need faster decisions under uncertainty.

What

Structured risk signals, confidence-scored alerts, historical replay, and workflow-ready outputs.

Why

Because disruption compounds quickly; the advantage is lead time with context, not raw data volume.

PRODUCTS

Strait-Signal

Atlas

Enterprise cross-domain dependency and cascade scoring that turns local alerts into prioritized systemic actions.
