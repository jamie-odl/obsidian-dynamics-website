WHAT IT MEASURES

Three signals. One node. Continuous proof.

Universal sensor ports feed a single edge pipeline. Thresholds are evaluated on-site against the limits that apply to your consent.

NOISE · LAeq

Noise monitoring

Measures: LAeq, LAmax and short-period levels in dB(A).

Continuous sound levels checked against BS 5228 day, evening and night limits — with working-hours filtering so out-of-hours events stand out.

VIBRATION · PPV

Vibration & seismic

Measures: Peak particle velocity (PPV) on three axes via geophone.

Triaxial ground-vibration capture evaluated against BS 7385 guidance, with breach detection and escalation when limits are approached.

POSITION · GPS

Location & geofence

Measures: GPS position, movement and site assignment.

Every node knows where it is. Auto site assignment, geofence alerts and a position trail keep your fleet mapped without manual admin.
